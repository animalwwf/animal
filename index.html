Common Questions

What is the mission of $ANIMAL?
Started by Vibhu, $ANIMAL is a memecoin with a genuine purpose. We are dedicated to supporting endangered species by ensuring 100% of fees are donated directly to the WWF.
How does the Donate breakdown work?
The distribution is fully transparent: 100% goes to the WWF, 20% is the dev's share, 10% is allocated to the team, and 5% is used for the $DISNEYFY token (the dev's main token ecosystem).
Is the contract safe?
Yes. Because we launched on pump.fun, the mint and freeze authorities are automatically revoked upon curve graduation, and all LP tokens are burned. The contract is immutable.
